Understanding Covered Call Option Strategies

Posted on November 10, 2025

Covered calls offer a disciplined and conservative way to earn income while holding stocks you already own. This strategy works best in neutral or slightly bullish markets and can be repeated to generate steady income.

Example: You hold 100 shares of XZY at $100 each, now trading at $110. You sell a call with a $115 strike for $1 premium. If the stock rises above $115, you sell at that level — locking in your target profit plus the premium. If it stays below, you keep both your shares and the premium.
